You can leave this quiz and return anytime by clicking , and your progress will be saved
On the quiz page, when you click , you will be taken to a page where you can see your downloadable/printable quiz report
On the navigation bar, when you click on Reports, you will see a list of historial quiz reports, including date and time taken, your score, and the link to generate your PDF report.
These reports can be customized in the Settings tab on the navigation sidebar. You can choose what to include in your score report, and you can customize other things like font size
This application includes an intelligent help feature powered by natural language processing. Click on in the bottom right corner of the screen, ask it a question, and it will respond with a list of questions and answers that match it.
You can view a list of frequently asked questions in the FAQs tab
